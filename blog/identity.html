Identity, Values, and Strengths

Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now

I stopped buying fast fashion after seeing the negative impacts it has in the environment and decided to thrift most of my clothes, I enjoy thrifting now its now a hobby of mine and I’ve been sharing my hobby with the people around me.

Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

Growing up, seeing my parents work hard helped me become more self-disciplined. My friends from different cultural backgrounds exposed me to different parts of the world which helped me to respect different cultures.

Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.

Being able to manage my time will allow me to effectively learn and not procrastinate.

Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

My biggest hurdle is communication I’m not a chatty person, and this hinders me by not being able to communicate my ideas to other peers or explain the problem that I’ve been stuck on I’m working on honing my communication skills.

Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.

When working with others I hope to get the easy parts and still end up being the anchor of the team I’ve been trying to challenge myself with harder task so I can pick up my own weight and contribute.
