Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is being aware of your own emotions and controlling them and being aware of others emotions.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is your emotional abilities and IQ is your cognitive abilities.

IQ represents abilities related to intelligence, including:

  • Visual processing
  • Logical and quantitative reasoning
  • Short- and long-term memory
  • Word comprehension
  • General knowledge

    • EQ is centred on abilities related to emotions or emotional perception, including:

  • Identifying emotions in themselves and others
  • Evaluating emotions in themselves and others
  • Controlling one's emotions.
  • Expressing one's emotions.
  • Using emotions in social situations
  • Relating others' emotions to one's own
